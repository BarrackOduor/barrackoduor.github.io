About Me

I’m Oduor Barrack,a graduate of Masinde Muliro University of Science and Technology with Bsc Mechanical and Industrial Engineering.I'm a registered Graduate Mechanical Engineer with the Engineers Board of Kenya (EBK), driven by a deep passion for Industrial Automation, Robotics and Process Optimization. I find joy in integrating mechanical systems with modern control technologies to make industries more efficient, reliable and sustainable.


My career started at Mabati Rolling Mills (MRM), where I worked as a Mechanical Maintenance Trainee. It was there that I first understood how theory meets real industrial systems, keeping machines running, improving efficiency and learning the rhythm of production lines. Later, I joined the Kenya Industrial Research and Development Institute (KIRDI) as a Mechanical Engineer Trainee,(R&D& Manufacturing Projects) where I experienced the innovation side of engineering turning ideas into practical industrial solutions.


Afterward, I joined Robotech Africa Limited as a Machine Specialist, where my true passion for industrial automation and robotics came alive. I worked on machine integration, control systems and process optimization, and began to see the incredible potential of merging mechanical systems with digital intelligence.


Currently, I am a MEP Mechanical Engineer at the State Department for Housing and Urban Development working on Affordable Housing Project. In this role, I support mechanical designs for affordable housing MEP systems including firefighting systems, water supply and reticulation networks, drainage systems, HVAC installations and mechanical site inspections. This experience has strengthened my ability to integrate mechanical building services with real-world construction demands, ensuring compliance with engineering standards, safety and sustainable infrastructure development.


Today, I’m committed to advancing automation solutions that drive sustainable industrial growth, not just in Kenya but across the world. My goal is to help industries embrace smart technologies, improve productivity, and inspire the next generation of engineers who believe in innovation with purpose.


Every system I design, every process I improve and every project I take on is guided by one mission to make industries smarter, greener and more efficient for a better tomorrow.
